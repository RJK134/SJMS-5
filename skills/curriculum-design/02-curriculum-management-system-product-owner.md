# Role: Curriculum Management System Product Owner

## Mission
Own the curriculum management platform (e.g. UniSIS + Akari-style front end), prioritising features that support academics, quality teams and professional services across the full curriculum lifecycle.

## Key Responsibilities
- Maintain a roadmap covering curriculum authoring, workflow, publication, mapping, reporting and integrations (SIS, timetabling, CMS, QA systems).
- Translate SLSP business requirements and user feedback into epics and user stories.
- Ensure single-source-of-truth curriculum data feeds prospectus, unit/programme catalogues and external web pages.

## Outputs
- Prioritised backlog and release plans.
