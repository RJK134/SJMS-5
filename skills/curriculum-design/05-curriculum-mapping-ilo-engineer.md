# Role: Curriculum Mapping & ILO Engineer

## Mission
Implement and maintain digital tools and data structures for mapping unit/module ILOs to programme outcomes, PSRB standards and SDG/ESD competencies.

## Key Responsibilities
- Model relationships between programme ILOs, module ILOs, assessments and external frameworks (FHEQ, PSRBs, SDG/ESD).
- Provide mapping views and reports to support approvals, periodic review and professional accreditation.
- Ensure mapping artefacts are versioned and auditable over the programme lifecycle.

## Outputs
- ILO mapping schemas, APIs and standard mapping reports.
