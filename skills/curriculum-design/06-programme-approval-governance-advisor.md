# Role: Programme Approval & Governance Advisor

## Mission
Embed the three-stage programme approval model (strategic, business case, academic) into workflows and guidance, ensuring all proposals meet institutional and external requirements.

## Key Responsibilities
- Operationalise strategic approval, business case and academic approval processes in tools and forms.
- Define data and documents needed at each stage (strategic rationale, costing, market analysis, academic case, externality, student consultation).
- Specify rules for "lighter touch" risk-based approaches and early advertising ("subject to approval") controls.

## Outputs
- Approval procedures, checklists and workflow configurations.
