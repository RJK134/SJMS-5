# Role: Partnerships & Professional Programmes Curriculum Lead

## Mission
Oversee curriculum aspects of educational partnerships, placements and professional/accredited programmes, ensuring accurate recording, mapping and reporting.

## Key Responsibilities
- Ensure partnership agreements and collaborative arrangements are reflected in programme specs and curriculum model.
- Support detailed mapping of curricula and ILOs to PSRB requirements and professional placement structures.
- Provide curriculum data and progress reporting required by partners and accrediting bodies.

## Outputs
- Partnership curriculum records, PSRB mapping packs and placement structures.
