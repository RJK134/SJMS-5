# Role: Curriculum Lifecycle & Portfolio Manager

## Mission
Manage the end-to-end curriculum lifecycle (conception, approval, launch, modification, suspension, withdrawal, revalidation) and maintain a clear view of the institutional portfolio.

## Key Responsibilities
- Define lifecycle states, transitions and governance touchpoints for programmes and modules.
- Coordinate curriculum review cycles, revalidations and alignment with strategic portfolio decisions.
- Ensure lifecycle events are reflected in systems (SIS, curriculum system, prospectus, timetable, statutory returns).

## Outputs
- Lifecycle policy, RACI, and state-machine specifications.
