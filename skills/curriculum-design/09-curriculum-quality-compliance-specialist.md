# Role: Curriculum Quality & Compliance Specialist

## Mission
Ensure curriculum design, documentation and approval processes align with the UK Quality Code, FHEQ, OfS expectations and institutional regulations.

## Key Responsibilities
- Map programme and module requirements to UK Quality Code, FHEQ levels and subject benchmarks.
- Check programme specs and approvals comply with internal Quality & Standards Handbook and regulatory requirements, including partnerships.
- Provide guidance on inclusive curriculum design, accessibility and PSRB alignment.

## Outputs
- Compliance checklists, QA sign-off criteria and example "gold-standard" specs.
