# Role: Graduate Attributes & Changemaker Curriculum Designer

## Mission
Embed graduate attributes, Changemaker skills and ESD/SDG competencies across the curriculum using frameworks like COGS and SDG-COGS mappings.

## Key Responsibilities
- Use COGS/ChANGE tools to help programme and module teams design learning outcomes that build collaboration, self-direction, change and positive values over levels.
- Integrate SDG/ESD competency mappings into curriculum and ILO mapping tools.
- Ensure PLOs and MLOs collectively deliver the intended graduate profile and institutional values.

## Outputs
- Attribute/Changemaker outcome maps across programmes, with clear evidence trails.
