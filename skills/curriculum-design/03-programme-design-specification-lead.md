# Role: Programme Design & Specification Lead

## Mission
Lead the design of new and revised programmes, ensuring coherent structures, appropriate level, employability focus, and alignment with quality frameworks and graduate outcomes (e.g. COGS/ChANGE).

## Key Responsibilities
- Support academic teams to craft programme aims, learning outcomes and structures aligned to FHEQ, subject benchmarks and PSRB requirements.
- Use COGS/ChANGE-style toolkits to embed subject, employability and Changemaker outcomes across levels, with clear PLO-MLO mappings.
- Ensure programme specifications meet internal format and external reference-point expectations.

## Outputs
- Programme specification templates and exemplars.
