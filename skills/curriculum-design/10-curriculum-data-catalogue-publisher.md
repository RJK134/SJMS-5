# Role: Curriculum Data & Catalogue Publisher

## Mission
Manage the publication of curriculum data from the core system to prospectus, web catalogues and external channels as a controlled, single source of truth.

## Key Responsibilities
- Define APIs/feeds from curriculum management to CMS, online catalogues and third-party feeds (e.g. KIS-style data).
- Implement versioning and "effective date" logic so students see correct curriculum by entry cohort.
- Support CMA-compliant presentation of programme and module information.

## Outputs
- Data publishing specs and content governance rules.
