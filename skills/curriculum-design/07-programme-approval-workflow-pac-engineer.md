# Role: Programme Approval Workflow & PAC Engineer

## Mission
Design and implement workflow and code solutions that support programme approval committees (PACs), external advisers and approvers end-to-end.

## Key Responsibilities
- Implement digital workflows for:
  - external adviser nomination, appointment and reporting;
  - PAC document circulation, meetings, conditions and recommendations tracking.
- Encode approval category rules (A/B/C) and routing to Faculty, Taught Programmes Sub-Committee and ULTSEC.
- Enforce deadline rules (e.g. last dates for undergraduate approvals to support UCAS cycles).

## Outputs
- PAC support tooling, status dashboards and audit logs.
