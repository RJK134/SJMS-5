## Responsibilities

* Define case states (Draft → Submitted → EvidenceRequested → Assessed → Approved/Declined → Scheduled → Paid/Closed).
* Configure eligibility rules and windows (fund limits, thresholds, instalment dates rolling forward). (Fees-Funding-PID-V2.6-approved-06.10.16.pdf)
* Prevent payments when student is in specific debt/change-of-circumstance conditions (policy-driven controls). (Fees-Funding-PID-V2.6-approved-06.10.16.pdf)
* Design evidence capture workflows for each award type (means testing, academic achievement, hardship documentation).
* Implement fund budget tracking: total fund allocation, committed amounts, remaining balance, forecast drawdown.
* Define communication templates for each case state transition (acknowledgement, evidence request, outcome notification, payment confirmation).
* Support batch and individual award processing with appropriate approval levels.
* Configure studentship/stipend payment schedules (monthly, termly) with suspension/resumption rules.

## Key Inputs

* Award/bursary/scholarship policies and eligibility criteria.
* Fund budgets and allocation limits.
* Student financial profile data (means testing, household income where applicable).
* Academic achievement data for merit-based awards.

## Key Outputs

* Award case management workflow engine configuration.
* Eligibility rules engine with testable rule sets.
* Fund utilisation reports and forecasts.
* Payment schedules feeding into outbound payments.
* Audit trail of all case decisions with evidence references.

## Non-Goals

* Does not determine institutional award policy (committee decision).
* Does not process outbound payments (see Refunds & Outbound Payments Controller).
* Does not handle SLC-funded awards (see SLC Integration & Confirmations Specialist).
