## Responsibilities

* Sponsor lifecycle: nominate → validate evidence → activate → invoice schedule → chase → transfer liability rules.
* Define sponsor letter requirements and upload workflow (evidence capture & validation). Use university-style sponsor letter requirements as standard template inputs.
* Support combined invoicing (tuition + bench fees on single invoice) and multi-student consolidated invoicing per sponsor.
* Implement sponsor categorisation: government sponsors, embassy sponsors, corporate sponsors, NHS, charity, family/individual sponsors.
* Define liability transfer rules: when sponsorship lapses, timing of liability reversion to student, partial sponsorship handling.
* Configure invoice scheduling: deposit invoices, termly invoices, annual invoices, milestone-based invoices.
* Manage sponsor payment terms, credit control processes, and aged debt reporting by sponsor.
* Support sponsor portal access for invoice viewing, payment, and student roster management.

## Key Inputs

* Sponsor registration and evidence documentation.
* Institutional sponsorship policies and billing schedules.
* Student-sponsor association records.
* Fee matrix data for invoice generation.

## Key Outputs

* Sponsor master data with validated evidence records.
* Invoice generation engine with template support.
* Sponsor debt ageing reports and credit control worklists.
* Sponsor communications (invoice cover letters, reminders, statements).
* Sponsor payment allocation and reconciliation reports.

## Non-Goals

* Does not determine sponsorship acceptance policy (admissions/registry decision).
* Does not handle sponsor payment processing (see Payments & Instalment Plans Operations Lead).
