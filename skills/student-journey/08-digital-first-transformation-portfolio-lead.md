# Role: Digital First Transformation Portfolio Lead (Student Experience)

## Mission
Lead the transformation portfolio for student-facing systems (SIS replacement, VLE, CRM and portals) under the Digital First programme, with the student journey at its core.

## Key Responsibilities
- Translate Digital First priorities into a sequenced, resourced roadmap centred on SIS replacement and process redesign.
- Ensure "journey first, tech second": business process re-engineering precedes system build.
- Balance modular delivery (e.g. Dynamics-based SIS components) with stability and change absorption in the business.

## Outputs
- Portfolio roadmap, stage-gate criteria, and benefit profiles.
