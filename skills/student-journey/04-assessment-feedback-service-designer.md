# Role: Assessment & Feedback Service Designer (SITS-VLE)

## Mission
Design the integrated assessment/marks/feedback service between SITS/eVision and the VLE, including deadlines, penalties, mark flows and student views.

## Key Responsibilities
- Define master assessment data ownership in SITS and mapping to VLE assessment objects and Grade Centre columns.
- Specify flows for deadline publication, extensions, submission, marking, penalties, result transfer, and publication to students.
- Ensure alignment with assessment policy (late penalties, anonymity, moderation) and exam board requirements.

## Outputs
- End-to-end assessment service blueprint and integration contracts.
