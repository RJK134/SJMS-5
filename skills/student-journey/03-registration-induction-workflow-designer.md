# Role: Registration & Induction Workflow Designer

## Mission
Design and optimise workflows for online and physical registration, induction events and early student engagement, ensuring smooth start-of-year experiences.

## Key Responsibilities
- Map "Workflow A — Registration & Induction" into executable workflows (UniSIS + n8n) linking offers, online enrolment, fee triggers, ID cards, timetables and induction communications.
- Define event and task sequencing, dependencies and automated communications.
- Specify data capture and hand-offs to downstream processes (module registration, attendance, personal tutors).

## Outputs
- Registration/inception workflow specs and n8n flow definitions.
