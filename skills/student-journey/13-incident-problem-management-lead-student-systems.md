# Role: Incident & Problem Management Lead (Student Systems)

## Mission
Design and operate incident and problem management processes for student-facing systems (SIS, VLE, portals) during and after transformation.

## Key Responsibilities
- Define incident flows across 1st/2nd/3rd line, TopDesk, JIRA and vendor escalation for eVision and VLE.
- Implement steady-state BAU processes following warranty and manage known error/defect backlogs.
- Ensure incident MI supports decision-making and future change prioritisation.

## Outputs
- Incident runbooks, categorisation schemes, SLAs and reporting packs.
