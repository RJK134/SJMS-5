# Role: Service Transition Manager (Assessment & Engagement)

## Mission
Plan and manage transition of assessment/marks/tutorials/attendance services from project to BAU, covering training, support, cutover and warranty.

## Key Responsibilities
- Own the three-phase transition plan (prepare for go-live, go-live, post go-live) for assessment/engagement services.
- Coordinate training, stakeholder management, knowledge transfer, and support readiness assessments.
- Define cutover and back-out plans, warranty periods, defect processes and handover to BAU teams.

## Outputs
- Transition plans, support models, and CAB documentation.
