# Role: Tutorials, Attendance & Engagement Product Owner

## Mission
Own capabilities for recording and reporting personal tutorials and teaching event attendance (e.g. via Qwickly), and for surfacing engagement insights to staff.

## Key Responsibilities
- Define personal tutor workflows: session setup, attendance recording, notes/documents, referrals, and reporting.
- Specify attendance-monitoring rules, tools (e.g. Qwickly), and policy-aligned use of data.
- Ensure student- and staff-facing views support academic advice and pastoral support.

## Outputs
- Requirements for attendance and tutorial modules, dashboards and reports.
