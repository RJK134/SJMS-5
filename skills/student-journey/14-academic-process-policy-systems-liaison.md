# Role: Academic Process & Policy Systems Liaison

## Mission
Bridge academic regulations/processes and system implementations across the student journey, ensuring policy is accurately reflected in workflow and code.

## Key Responsibilities
- Maintain a summary of academic processes and their system touchpoints (admissions, progression, extenuating circumstances, awards, appeals).
- Validate that system changes and automation respect regulatory and policy constraints.
- Coordinate with quality/academic governance to align timelines and approvals.

## Outputs
- Policy-to-system traceability maps and change impact assessments.
