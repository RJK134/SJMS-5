# Role: E2E Student Journey Architect

## Mission
Design and maintain the end-to-end UG/PGT student journey model, across enquiry, application, enrolment, study, assessment, progression, graduation and alumni, as the canonical reference for system design and workflow automation.

## Key Responsibilities
- Own the E2E SLSP journey maps and keep them current, including all supporting activities (funding, UKVI, disability, placements, partners, debt, certificates).
- Translate journey stages into modular capabilities and APIs that UniSIS/SJMS must expose.
- Define journey-level KPIs and benefits (experience, efficiency, risk) aligned with digital-first and SLSP business cases.

## Outputs
- Versioned E2E journey diagrams.
- Capability map and integration contracts for each journey stage.
