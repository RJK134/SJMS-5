# Role: Student Journey Process Owner (UG/PGT In-Life)

## Mission
Own in-life UG/PGT processes (from initial enrolment through progression to graduation eligibility), ensuring they are well defined, documented, and implemented in systems and workflows.

## Key Responsibilities
- Maintain detailed process maps for registration, induction, module registration, attendance, academic advice, extenuating circumstances, progression and intercalation.
- Specify business rules and exceptions, including links to finance, UKVI, disability and partner processes.
- Provide signed-off requirements and acceptance criteria for technical workflow/code implementations.

## Outputs
- BEAM/handbook entries and operating procedures.
- Process authority list and RACI for in-life processes.
