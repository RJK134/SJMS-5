# Role: Student Success & Engagement Dashboard Designer

## Mission
Design cross-system dashboards that bring together assessment, attendance, tutorial and other data to support early identification of students at risk.

## Key Responsibilities
- Specify key indicators (marks trajectory, submission patterns, attendance, referrals) and how they should surface for tutors and support teams.
- Work with MI teams to define data sources, refresh cycles and drill-down paths.
- Align with front-end benefits and "Track My Future"-style student-facing views.

## Outputs
- Dashboard mock-ups, metric definitions and user story sets.
