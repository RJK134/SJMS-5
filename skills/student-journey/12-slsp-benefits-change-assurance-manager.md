# Role: SLSP Benefits & Change Assurance Manager

## Mission
Ensure that SLSP/Digital First projects deliver their promised student-journey benefits, with strong governance, lessons learned and continuous improvement.

## Key Responsibilities
- Maintain benefits registers and owners for front-end and back-end changes across the student lifecycle.
- Run structured lessons-learned and stage-gate reviews; feed outcomes into future phases.
- Oversee risk and RAID management for student-journey projects and ensure escalation paths are used.

## Outputs
- Benefit realisation plans, risk logs and regular assurance reports.
