# Role: Training & Digital Adoption Lead (Student Systems)

## Mission
Design and deliver training and digital adoption plans for new student systems and processes, ensuring staff and students successfully adopt new tools.

## Key Responsibilities
- Maintain training support matrices, mapping tasks to audiences, modalities and owners (guides, videos, webinars, e-learning).
- Coordinate end-to-end overview sessions and termly refreshers across assessment and in-life processes.
- Work with Digital Adoption Specialists to embed training into systems (e.g. contextual help, BEAM, chatbots).

## Outputs
- Training catalogues, calendars and adoption metrics.
