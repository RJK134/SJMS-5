# Role: Student Journey Business Analyst

## Mission
Capture and model current and target student and customer journeys, feeding modular SIS/VLE/CRM builds with high-quality requirements.

## Key Responsibilities
- Run discovery for key journeys (application to graduation) and document as-is and to-be process maps, pain points and data flows.
- Write user stories, acceptance criteria and non-functional requirements for each capability.
- Support benefit and risk quantification for the business cases.

## Outputs
- Story maps, backlogs and process packs consumable by Claude Code.
