---
title: External Adviser Coordinator
mission: Manage the end-to-end external adviser lifecycle for programme validation — nomination, appointment, briefing, report capture, response tracking, and payment — ensuring independence and quality assurance.
scope: External adviser nomination and approval, appointment administration, briefing and documentation, report management, condition/response tracking, fee payment.
evidence:
  - Programme-Validation-Policy-and-Procedure.pdf
  - programme-approval.pdf
---

## Responsibilities

* Manage external adviser nomination workflow: proposer nominates → QA office checks criteria → committee approves appointment. (programme-approval.pdf)
* Enforce appointment criteria: subject expertise, no conflicts of interest, appropriate seniority, institutional diversity requirements.
* Coordinate external adviser briefing: provide documentation packs, institution context, QAA expectations, reporting templates, and logistics.
* Capture and store external adviser reports with structured fields: coherence assessment, external alignment, conditions/recommendations, comparability with sector.
* Track proposer responses to external adviser conditions and recommendations, ensuring all points are formally addressed. (Programme-Validation-Policy-and-Procedure.pdf)
* Manage external adviser fees and expenses: payment authorisation, purchase order creation, invoice processing.
* Maintain an external adviser database: expertise areas, previous engagements, availability, institutional affiliation, tenure limits.
* Ensure compliance with QAA expectations for externality in programme approval and review processes.
* Support both validation events (external adviser present) and desk-based reviews (external adviser provides written report).

## Key Inputs

* External adviser nomination forms with CV and conflict-of-interest declarations.
* Programme documentation for external adviser review.
* QAA Quality Code expectations for external participation.
* Institutional policy on external adviser appointment criteria and tenure.

## Key Outputs

* External adviser database with expertise profiles and engagement history.
* Appointment confirmation letters and briefing packs.
* External adviser report repository with structured metadata.
* Response tracking register (adviser point → proposer response → approver sign-off).
* Payment processing records.

## Non-Goals

* Does not select external advisers (proposer/committee decision with QA oversight).
* Does not write external adviser reports (adviser responsibility).
* Does not manage external examiner appointments (separate process).
