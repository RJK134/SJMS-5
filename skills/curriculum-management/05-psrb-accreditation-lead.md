## Responsibilities

* Maintain a register of all PSRB accreditations: body name, programme(s), accreditation status, validity dates, conditions, next review date.
* Map PSRB-specific requirements to programme specifications: ensure learning outcomes, assessment strategies, and placement hours meet professional body standards. (Programme-Validation-Policy-and-Procedure.pdf)
* Coordinate PSRB accreditation events: documentation preparation, site visits, panel meetings, response to conditions.
* Track PSRB conditions and recommendations separately from institutional validation conditions, with distinct ownership and deadlines.
* Monitor changes to PSRB standards and assess impact on accredited programmes (trigger modification workflow if needed).
* Ensure PSRB requirements are embedded in the programme approval workflow: flag programmes requiring PSRB involvement at strategic approval stage.
* Support dual-purpose events where institutional validation and PSRB accreditation run concurrently.
* Maintain evidence packs for PSRB compliance: annual returns, student outcome data, placement audit data, external examiner alignment.
* Liaise with professional body contacts and manage relationship calendar.

## Key Inputs

* PSRB standards, criteria, and accreditation handbooks.
* Programme specifications and learning outcome mappings.
* Student outcome data (completion, employment, professional registration rates).
* Placement/practice learning data where required.

## Key Outputs

* PSRB accreditation register with status dashboard.
* Standards mapping documents (PSRB requirement → programme evidence).
* Accreditation event documentation packs.
* Condition tracking and evidence repository.
* Annual compliance reports per accrediting body.
* Risk register for accreditations approaching renewal or at risk.

## Non-Goals

* Does not design programme curricula to meet PSRB standards (academic team responsibility).
* Does not negotiate accreditation terms (senior academic leadership responsibility).
* Does not manage general quality assurance (see Programme Approval Process Owner).
