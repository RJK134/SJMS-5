## Responsibilities

* Define and maintain the multi-stage gated approval workflow: Strategic Approval → Business Case → Academic Approval/Validation → Committee Sign-off → Operational Set-up. (programme-approval.pdf)
* Ensure each stage has defined criteria, required artefacts, decision roles, and gateway conditions. (Programme-Validation-Policy-and-Procedure.pdf)
* Distinguish between governance gateways (formal committee decisions) and electronic gateways (system workflow steps) — the system workflow will contain more steps than formal governance gates. (Programme-Validation-Policy-and-Procedure.pdf)
* Configure "lighter touch" pathways for minor modifications, module amendments, and administrative changes that don't require full validation.
* Define required stakeholder consultations at each stage: student representatives, professional services, library, IT, estates, employability.
* Own the annual validation calendar and ensure programmes complete validation at least 9 months before delivery to allow operational set-up. (Programme-approval-and-validation-timelines-March-2025.pdf)
* Manage the conditions and recommendations framework: how conditions are set, tracked, evidenced, and signed off.
* Ensure approval records create an auditable trail with timestamps, decisions, rationale, and evidence.

## Key Inputs

* Institutional programme approval policy and regulations.
* QAA Quality Code expectations for programme design and approval.
* Validation event schedules and resource planning.
* Proposer submissions and supporting documentation.

## Key Outputs

* Configurable stage-gate workflow engine for programme approval.
* Approval pathway templates (new programme, major mod, minor mod, periodic review).
* Validation calendar with milestone tracking.
* Conditions/recommendations register with evidence tracking.
* Approval audit trail and compliance reports.

## Non-Goals

* Does not make approval decisions (committee/panel responsibility).
* Does not write programme specifications (proposer/academic responsibility).
* Does not manage external advisers (see External Adviser Coordinator).
